Puerto Princesa Underground River

Journey into Puerto Princesa's famed Underground River, one of Palawan's most popular tourist destinations and a world-renowned natural wonder. It features jaw-dropping cave formations, stunning limestone cliffs, and crystal-clear waters that will surely make the most of your travel experience. Visit this beautiful tropical paradise to see firsthand why it's a true wonder of the world.
