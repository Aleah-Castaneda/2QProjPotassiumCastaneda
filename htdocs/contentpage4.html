Blood Compact Shrine

The Blood Compact Shrine was created to commemorate a friendship alliance in Philippine history between Miguel Lopez de Legazpi of Spain and Datu Sikatuna of Bohol. Visitors can have a good view of the sea and contour of that side of the island of Bohol by standing on the dais. It is located along a national highway with numerous shops offering souvenirs and gifts both along and across the highway.
